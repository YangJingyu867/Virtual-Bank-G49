⁣⁣⁣⁣　Industrial Light and Magic (ILM) is a famous film special effects production company. Ilm has provided visual effects services for more than 300 films. ILM's wonders are not limited to the many amazing CG and visual effects created in many films, but rather to the fact that it ushered in a new era in the film special effects industry, and ILM has won 15 Academy Awards for Best Visual Effects. Today, Industrial Light & Magic still represents the world's top production standards in the special effects industry, and its technical achievements are unmatched. "Film is more important than technology" - this concept has evolved into an important tenet of ILM and has led it to continue to grow and develop.

Jaeger-CHERNO ALPHA

The ugliest mecha, but the most powerful. Roughly detailed, with a strong foundation in heavy industry, it takes an unconventional approach, similar to the rugged T-34 tanks of the former Soviet Union and the Russian robots of Red Alert during World War II. The hand discharge device of this model is a highlight, and the cockpit is moved to the chest. The huge device on the head is used for charging, and the thick head armor can also serve as bait for monster attacks.

Jaeger-COYOTE TANGO

The design style of the coyote tango is a reference to the AH-1 Cobra gunship. The head is a two-person independent cockpit, which is also an emergency rescue capsule, and can be ejected as a whole in a crisis. On either side of the chest are radioactive vents. The abdomen is a polyhedral carcass joint. The feet are made of steel polymer, which enhances the durability of body movement. On the shoulder are two heavy mortars.
